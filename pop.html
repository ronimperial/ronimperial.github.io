Populism Questions

2.10 Students will determine the economic, social and political, causes and effects of the populist and progressive movements by utilizing one of the big 11 social studies skills.

2.11 Students will analyze major social movements in the United States and explain their impact on the changing social and political culture, i.e., the populist and progressive movements by utilizing one of the big 11 social studies skills.

My populism questions sheet meets standard 2.10 as well as 2.11, because it determines the economic, social, and political causes and effects of the populist as well as progressive movements. It also utilized major social movements in the United States, because it tells the impact of the Populist movement on the economy and America itself. I used 20th Century Skills (using techonology for research and using writing skills) in order to make this product.

It relates to the 21st Century because the problems that the Populist movement made back then resonates with the problems we have today.
